Interstellar Movie Review

	Interstellar is a 2014 science fiction film co-written, directed and produced by Christopher Nolan.
	It stars Matthew McConaughey, Anne Hathaway, Jessica Chastain, Bill Irwin, Ellen Burstyn, Matt Damon, and Michael Caine.
	Set in a dystopian future where humanity is struggling to survive, the film follows a group of astronauts who travel through a wormhole near
	Saturn in search of a new home for mankind.
